Why Does the Sky Look Blue?

Sunlight contains all colors of visible light.

As sunlight passes through Earth's atmosphere, blue wavelengths are scattered more strongly than longer wavelengths.

This scattering causes the sky to appear blue.

The Seven-Day Week

Most modern calendars divide time into seven-day weeks.

The days are: Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday.

This system has existed for centuries and is used globally.

What Makes an Animal a Mammal?

  • Breathes air using lungs
  • Warm-blooded
  • Produces milk for offspring
  • Has hair or fur at some point in life

Dolphins are mammals even though they live in water.
